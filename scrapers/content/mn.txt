               <h1>
                Registered Doulas
               </h1>
               <p>
                <strong>
                 Please Note:
                </strong>
                Doulas listed on the Registry have met the requirements in Minnesota Statutes, section
                <a href="https://www.revisor.mn.gov/statutes/cite/148.995">
                 148.995
                </a>
                to
                <a href="https://www.revisor.mn.gov/statutes/cite/148.997">
                 148.997
                </a>
                .
               </p>
               <p>
                Criminal history checks are completed by MDH via public access to the Minnesota Bureau of Criminal Apprehension (BCA). Information about the criminal background check process required by Minnesota Statutes, section
                <a href="https://www.revisor.mn.gov/statutes/cite/148.996">
                 148.996
                </a>
                is available through the BCA website:
                <a href="https://chs.state.mn.us/">
                 Minnesota Public Criminal History Search
                </a>
                .
               </p>
